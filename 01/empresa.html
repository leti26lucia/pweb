EMPRESA

Founded in March 1968 by Ozwell Spencer, James Marcus, and Edward Ashford, the Umbrella Corporation was a large pharmaceutical conglomerate with laboratories and bases spread across almost every corner of the world, but with a greater concentration in Europe. Officially, Umbrella developed a wide range of pharmaceutical products, but unofficially, the corporation's true objective and focus was the development of biological weapons and genetic engineering technology. This is precisely what gave Umbrella immense wealth and influence in virtually every sphere of power, especially after becoming a member of the executive board of the Global Pharmaceutical Consortium.
